ASK YOUR QUESTIONS
1. How can I get admission to a university in USA?

- Research universities and programs.

- Take required standardized tests (like the SAT or ACT) unless applying to test-optional schools.

- Prepare necessary documents (transcripts, recommendation letters, etc.).

- Complete the university application process.

2. Can I go to the USA for higher studies without taking the IELTS or SAT?
Yes, some universities in the USA offer admission without the requirement of IELTS or SAT scores. Look for universities that have a test-optional or flexible admissions policy.
3. Which country is safe and better for higher study?
Countries like Canada, Australia, UK, Germany, and USA are considered safe and offer excellent educational opportunities for Nepali students.
4. Which country offer PR?
Countries like USA, Canada, Germany, and Australia may offer pathways to Permanent Residency or work visas for skilled professionals after completing their studies and securing employment.
5. Is it possible to get a scholarship with a low cumulative GPA?
Yes, there are scholarship opportunities available that consider factors beyond GPA. Some scholarships focus on extracurricular activities, leadership skills, or specific talents. We can help you explore these options and find scholarships that match your strengths.
6. Do you provide accommodation assistance for students studying abroad?
Absolutely! We assist students in finding suitable accommodation options, ensuring a comfortable and safe living environment during their studies abroad.
7. Do you provide other visas besides student visas?
Absolutely! In addition to assisting students with their study abroad journeys, we also operate as a travel agency under the name "Bidesh App Pvt Ltd" Our services extend beyond student visas to include tourist visa processes for various countries. Whether you're planning a leisure trip or business travel, we're here to facilitate a smooth and hassle-free visa application process, ensuring you have the support you need for all your travel endeavors.
